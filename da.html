Rich Heritage

Founded in 1676 by Guru Ram Rai, Dehradun has evolved from a spiritual sanctuary to Uttarakhand's dynamic capital. The city's name reflects its rich history - 'Dehra' (camp) of Guru Ram Rai and 'Dun' (valley), nestled in the Doon Valley.

  • Ancient Buddhist temples and monasteries
  • British-era architecture
  • Historical freedom movement sites
